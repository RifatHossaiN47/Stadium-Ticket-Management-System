Instructions to Purchase Tickets

  • Tickets can be bought online ten days in advance.

  • You can pay for the tickets using mobile financial services: bKash, Nagad or Rocket Other payment options will be available soon.

  • In case of payment or transaction failure, the deducted amount would be refunded by your MFS provider within 8 business days.

  • In case money has been deducted from your mobile wallet but you have not received a ticket confirmation, the deducted amount would be refunded by your MFS provider within 8 business days.

  • If you have not received your ticket copy in email, kindly check your Spam / Junk folder.

  • In case of customers use fake website which claim to sell tickets of this stadium, the authorities will not take any liability.
